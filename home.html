What We Do

Our Services

Family Offices

For single or multi-family offices, we offer assistance in managing estate, investments, inheritance, zakat, and charitable activities according to Islamic principles.

Capital Market

We can help you design novel Islamic financial structures that are core to capital market activities and will assist in realizing new paradigms.

Payment Solutions

We specialize in resolving your Sharia banking issues and transforming your operational frameworks, including Sharia governance, audit, and control functions.

Funding Companies

Leveraging financial expertise and scientific resources, we align product structures and certifications for diverse opportunities.

Dispute Resolution

Mediation and legal assistance for resolving disputes and conflicts within the framework of Islamic law, promoting peaceful resolutions.

Halal Certification

Expert guidance and support for obtaining Halal certification for products and businesses, meeting the requirements of Islamic dietary laws.
